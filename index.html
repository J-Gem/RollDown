Features:

The ground Idea of the game is to combine the simplicity of the rolling ball genre with the thrill of the racing genre.


It will feature a big number of fictive tracks and levels in mostly real location world inspired, some of them will have the focus on giving the player a challenge wile others will provide great racing action.


Select from a wide selection of sport balls, together with an material editor it allows for a creative freedom.

Play three different game modes!

  • Race agents other opponents that react on your play style.
  • Time Attack survive the countdown and reach the checkpoints before the times runs out.
  • Speed-Trap beat the speed of the opponents on some high-speed tracks.

Carrier and multiplayer

  • In the Carrier there will be featured a wide selection of events, tracks and a continuously rivalry with your opponents.
  • The Multiplayer will be added later and will give you the change to compete in races and some party games online agents your friends and strangers.
